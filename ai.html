AI Technique: Bayesian Reasoning

Bayesian reasoning is a probabilistic technique used in Artificial Intelligence to update the probability of a hypothesis when new evidence is observed.

Bayes Theorem

P(Disease | Symptoms) = P(Symptoms | Disease) × P(Disease) ------------------------------------ P(Symptoms)

In this system, the symptoms entered by the user are used to estimate the probability of diseases. Based on this probability, the system suggests a treatment plan with confidence.
