Industry Problem Context

In healthcare systems, doctors must analyze patient symptoms and determine the most appropriate treatment plan. Manual diagnosis can sometimes be time-consuming and may lead to incorrect decisions.

AI systems can assist doctors by analyzing symptoms and estimating disease probability. This helps in faster and more accurate treatment planning.

Feasibility Study

The system uses probabilistic reasoning to analyze symptoms. It calculates the probability of diseases based on observed symptoms such as fever, cough and fatigue.
